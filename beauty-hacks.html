Skin care tips and tricks

  • Wash your pillows regularly as build up of dirt, oil and dead skin can lead to break outs.

  • Moderate your showers, as long hot showers can dry up the skin, leading to the use of more lotion than you'd otherwise need.

  • Use a two-in-one makup remover and cleanser - ensure you remove your makeup before you sleep.

  • A spoon full of sugar mixed in with lemon juice and honey makes for a cost effective exfoliating scrub.

Makeup tips and tricks

  • Are you struggling to find the right foundation for your skin tone? Do all the best options seem beyond your price range? Why not try mixing a lighter and darker shade from more economical brands to get the perfect tone.

  • It can be demoralizing seeing makeup tutorials with an entire pallette of eyeshadow colors. What you could do is repurpose the makeup you already have. A hashtag drawn with pencil eyeliner can produce the smoky eye effect. Old lipstick can also be used if you want to create more rosy shades.

  • Speaking of lipstick. What if one has the opposite problem? Eyeshadow powder can be mixed with vaseline for a stunning glossy lip shade.

  • Adding salt water to a drying/emptying tube of mascara can help it last longer.
